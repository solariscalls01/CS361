This quiz program is designed for learning Japanese vocabulary.
The quiz format is multiple-choice.  You will be presented with a Japanese word
written in Japanese and 4 options in English.  Your task is to select the correct English
option for the given Japanese word.  There are 3 levels for this quiz, beginner,
intermediate, and advanced. At the beginner level, a Japanese word is
given in Hiragana. In the intermediate level, Japanese word is presented in both Hiragana
and Katakana.  In the advanced level, the Japanese word includes Kanji characters.
After entering your selection and pressing Enter, the correct answer will be displayed.
There are 20 questions for each level, and at the end of the quiz set,
your total score will be shown.
